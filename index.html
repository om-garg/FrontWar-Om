Gwalior

Ensconced in the heart of Madhya Pradesh, the royal city of Gwalior stands in stately splendour with a stunning hilltop fort and the resplendent Jai Vilas Palace. With a scattering of heritage structures, Gwalior is also the gateway to the wild heart of India, within which are hidden several dense forests and tiger reserves. Interestingly, Gwalior's history is rooted in a legend.
